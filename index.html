An invitation to all SUCs from Luzon with ICT-related bachelor's degree program

While it is important to focus in preparing the future generations of farmers and other agriculture stakeholders, it is equally important to equip the future ICT innovators that will support them. AgriHackathon 3 calls on to the pillars of ICT education in the country to become partners in molding young minds as ICT-AgrInnovators. As the direction of global agriculture leans toward precision thru the use of ICT, they will be one of the key players who will take part in making the Philippines ahead of other developing countries in producing enough and affordable food.

OBJECTIVE:

To equip the future ICT-AgrInnovators with knowledge, experience, and motivation to develop ICT support for agriculture by:

  • Challenging and recognizing ideas, creativity, and innovation skills
  • Educating them about the direction of Philippine agriculture and the different opportunities in ICT for agriculture
  • Promoting business startups on ICT services for agriculture

Competitions

  • There will be 2 hacking competitions: Open Idea Battle and Closed Box Challenge.
  • Form a team of 3 students for each of the two competitions. A student can participate only in one competition. Both competitions are required to have an accompanying instructor-coach
  • Registration starts on October 1 and ends on November 8, 2016. Each competition is limited to 50 participants only so register as early as possible.
  • SUCs have the option to send participants to either or both competitions.

OPEN IDEA BATTLE

  • This competition seeks to sponsor further development of promising ideas among students.
  • Submit your concept note for pre-selection process. Entries must be either an existing thesis, project output, or new idea originated from the student-members of the team. Wait for acceptance confirmation on the next working day. Have the working prototype ready on the event date.
  • Entries will be judged based on 2 main criteria - product concept applicability in agriculture; and usability of the prototype.
  • Prizes:
    • 1st place: PhP100,000.00 worth of thesis or project assistance + PhP9,000.00 cash + medals + perks
    • 2nd place: PhP6,000.00 cash + medals + perks
    • 3rd place: PhP4,500.00 cash + medals + perks
    • The 2nd and 3rd place entries will also be considered for possible funding.

CLOSED BOX CHALLENGE

  • This challenge will test the competitiveness of your students against other schools in terms of understanding the client-users and providing solution to them.
  • A challenge kit will be uploaded at the event website on October 6, 2016 to provide enough time for hackers to develop their working prototype of the app.
  • Teams will compete for the best implementation of the app according to the defined specification on the challenge kit.
  • Outputs will be judged according to the execution of the requirements and the creativity of the app. If participants added some features which are not in the specified design, it will be the judgesâ€™ discretion to add extra points.
  • Prizes:
    • 1st place: PhP90,000.00 worth of internship program for 2 months + PhP9,000.00 cash + medals + perks
    • 2nd place: PhP6,000.00 cash + medals + perks
    • 3rd place: PhP4,500.00 cash + medals + perks

Aside from competitions, there will also be a seminar and open forum discussing the trends and opportunities in Agri-based ICT.
